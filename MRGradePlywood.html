Thylanium MR Grade Plywood is engineered for projects where strength, stability, finish, and everyday reliability matter most. "When the goal is strong interiors with refined finishing, Thylanium MR Grade Plywood is the material professionals trust."

What is MR Grade Plywood?

MR stands for Moisture Resistant. It is specially developed for interior environments where plywood may be exposed to normal humidity and occasional moisture, but not continuous water contact. This makes it an ideal solution for indoor furniture and interior spaces that require durability, dimensional stability, and a clean finish.
