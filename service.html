Uncompromising Quality

Our 7-Step Manufacturing Process

At Thylanium, we follow a refined process, adding two extra quality stages beyond the conventional method to ensure superior strength and consistency.

1

Timber Selection

Carefully selected logs to ensure strong and reliable core material from the very start.

2

Precision Veneer Peeling

Logs are peeled into uniform veneers to maintain absolute structural consistency.

3

Controlled Seasoning

Veneers are moisture-balanced to prevent any future warping, bending or deformation.

4

Core Layer Composition

Veneers are strategically layered and cross-bonded to enhance maximum strength and stability.

5

Advanced Adhesive Bonding

High-performance resins ensure an unbreakable bond between every single layer.

Extra Stage
6

Inspection & Calibration

An essential quality step to verify perfect layer alignment and core bonding before the final press.

7

High-Pressure Pressing

Sheets are hot pressed and finished with a final thickness and surface quality check.

This extra attention to process is what gives Thylanium its dependable strength — Truly the Teak of Plywood.
