LAYERED WITH LEGACY

About Thylanium

Thylanium was built with a simple but powerful vision — to redefine what plywood should represent in the modern market. In an industry where many choices are driven only by price, we chose to focus on what truly matters: strength, consistency, durability, and long-term value.

|We believe plywood is more than a sheet material. It is the hidden foundation behind furniture, interiors, workspaces, and homes.

The quality of that foundation determines how well the final result performs over time. That belief is what gave birth to Thylanium.

Our Story

Every great structure begins with a strong core. The same principle applies to plywood.

From the beginning, Thylanium was created to bring together advanced manufacturing methods, dependable raw materials, and uncompromising quality standards into one trusted product. Our goal was not simply to manufacture plywood, but to create a brand professionals could rely on with confidence.

By combining precision engineering with carefully selected timber and modern bonding technology, we developed plywood that performs beyond expectations — sheet after sheet, project after project.

Behind the Boards

Our Manufacturing Philosophy

At Thylanium, quality is never accidental. It is the exact result of rigorous discipline, precision process, and an unwavering attention to detail.

Advanced Production

Precision Engineering

This unyielding commitment to process is what gives Thylanium its trusted reputation.
