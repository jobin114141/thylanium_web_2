About Thylanium

Thylanium was created with a clear vision — to elevate the standard of plywood in India.

While most plywood focuses only on price competition, Thylanium focuses on performance, strength, and long-term reliability. Our manufacturing process combines advanced bonding technology, strict quality control, and carefully selected raw materials to ensure every sheet performs consistently.

|The result is plywood that professionals trust and customers appreciate.

Today, Thylanium is becoming the preferred choice of architects, interior designers, contractors, and skilled carpenters who believe that the foundation of good project begins with good plywood.

Why Choose Thylanium?

We refuse to compromise on quality. Every sheet that leaves our facility is crafted for longevity, precision, and ultimate strength. Here is what sets us apart.

PURE STRENGTH

Superior Core Strength

Built using carefully selected timber and structured core layers that enhance load-bearing strength and long-term stability.

Advanced Bonding Technology

High-performance adhesive bonding ensures better layer integration, minimizing risk of separation, bubbles, or delamination.

Precision Manufacturing

Every sheet is produced with strict process control to maintain absolute consistency in thickness, strength, and finish.

Carpenter Friendly

Thylanium plywood cuts cleaner, holds screws stronger, and reduces wastage — making it a favorite among professionals.
